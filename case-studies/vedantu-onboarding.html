What does "activated" actually mean? Defining Vedantu's first-7-day contract with the student.

Activation is the most abused word in growth. For a high-consideration, ₹80k-ASP purchase like Vedantu, the definition has to be specific, testable, and tied to the business model — not to product vanity.

Each ICP reaches the AHA moment via a different path. A single activation definition can't serve all three — but a single activation framework can.

Five activation hypotheses, stress-tested

The question isn't "what do users do early." It's "what do they do early that predicts retention, referral, and LTV."

HypothesisRetention?Referral?LTV?
Watch 7 master classes in 5 days✓✓✓
Ask 20 doubts in 5 days✓✓—
Buy a short-term course (<₹5k) in 7 days✓—✓
Enrol in a paid trial in 7 days✓✓✓
Take 5 mock tests in 10 days—✓—
Winner: Enrol in a paid trial within 7 days — the only hypothesis that hits all three downstream metrics. Paid trial is the penultimate step to the long-term course; someone paying for a 15-day trial in Week 1 is signalling commitment and has already crossed the value gate.

Metrics — quantitative

Output (effectiveness)

  • D1 / D7 / D30 retention
  • MAU / DAU
  • Time-to-activation (signup → onboarding → first milestone)
  • Activation rate by acquisition channel (YT, paid, referral, SEO)

Input (efficiency)

  • First-15-day attendance for paid courses
  • In-class rating + master-teacher rating
  • Average duration in master class & paid-trial classes (stickiness)
  • App latency, screen load time, form load, video load, CTA click rates on sign-up + activation screens
  • Paid-trial sign-up rate vs. onward retention rate
  • User cohort by Grade / Board / Stream / Tenure + demographics
  • Referral by cohort / K-factor

Metrics — qualitative

  • NPS / CSAT
  • App & Play Store reviews
  • Sentiment analysis on YouTube, Telegram, and parent testimonials at PTMs

Takeaway

For a high-ASP, multi-stakeholder purchase, the right activation metric is the one closest to the paywall — not the one easiest to hit. Master classes and doubts are great engagement; a paid trial is the only early signal that maps to real revenue. Design onboarding to push that lever, and measure everything else as inputs to it.
