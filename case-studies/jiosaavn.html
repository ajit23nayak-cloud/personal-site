Session duration 1.5 minutes vs. Spotify's 9. Why JioSaavn has an engagement problem — and how to fix it.

JioSaavn has the catalogue, the regional-language moat, and a huge SEO funnel from lyrics. The problem isn't top-of-funnel. It's what happens after the user lands.

The right engagement framework

Three options — pick by stage and strategic intent:

FrameworkTracksVerdict
FrequencySessions / weekSecondary — use as activation proxy
DepthTime in streaming, songs liked, playlists createdPrimary
BreadthCross-app use (Jio TV, Cinema, Meet)Tertiary — unlock at 300M+ active users
Depth wins. More time = better algorithmic capture = better recommendations = Saavn becomes primary. It's the only framework that compounds.

Define the active user

A user who, after signing up, streams one full song or 5 minutes — whichever comes first. The sign-up gate matters: a huge slice of current traffic plays without login, which is why session time averages 1.5 min and bounce is high.

Two product hooks that actually move depth

Hook 1 — Song & lyrics finder (India-first Shazam + Genius)

Shazam and Genius are weak on Indian and regional music. JioSaavn owns the catalogue. A detector + lyrics-search + karaoke-sync built natively into the app plugs into JioSaavn's single largest organic acquisition source: lyrics search. Match rate > 85% for Indian regional should be the bar.

Hook 2 — Playlist import from competitors

Paid apps like FreeYourMusic cost ₹1–3k/yr to port playlists — more than a JioSaavn subscription. Make it free inside the app, with a "import & win Pro" campaign: 1 week of Pro per song imported, capped at 1 year. This is a direct attack on switching friction — the real moat Spotify has over you.

Retention — negative actions to watch

Music-streaming D30 is 60–80% industry-typical. The first-month cliff is sharp. Watch these signals before the user opens the uninstall flow:

  • Sudden drop in session time or frequency.
  • User deletes or tries to export a playlist.
  • Support tickets: "how to export playlist", "how to get refund", "cancel subscription".
  • Deletes downloaded songs to free phone storage.
  • Music discovery stagnates — no new playlists / songs / genres in last 2 weeks.
  • Removes platform integrations (ringtone, Chrome, Mac).
  • Master subscription (Jio Fiber, ACT) nearing expiry.

Takeaway

Music streaming is a depth-of-engagement game, not a catalogue game. JioSaavn's catalogue is already world-class. The leverage is in: forcing the sign-up, rewarding the first session, and making the switching cost to JioSaavn lower than the switching cost away.
